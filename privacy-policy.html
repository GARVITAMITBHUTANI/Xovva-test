Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 24, 2024
At XOVVA Pharmaceuticals, we adhere to the highest standards of clinical data protection and patient confidentiality. This document outlines our protocols for data collection, processing, and safeguarding in compliance with relevant healthcare and privacy regulations.

Data Collection

We collect precise, compartmentalized data streams necessary for the execution of professional clinical services and product efficacy tracking. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • adjust
    Identity & Professional Credentials Data such as Medical License Numbers (NPI), clinic affiliations, and contact nomenclature for verified practitioners.
  • adjust
    Patient Efficacy Telemetry Anonymized, aggregate dermatological response metrics utilized strictly within clinical evidence protocols.
  • adjust
    Digital Interaction Logs Technical vectors including IP addresses, session duration, and interface interaction paths to optimize portal stability.

How We Use Your Information

Data utilization is strictly governed by clinical utility and operational necessity. We do not engage in unauthorized secondary commercialization of practitioner or patient data profiles.

  • Validation: To verify professional licensure prior to granting access to Rx-strength formulations and clinical dossiers.
  • Pharmacovigilance: To monitor product safety profiles, adverse event reporting, and longitudinal efficacy studies.
  • Communication: To disseminate critical formulation updates, clinical trial results, and protocol modifications.

Patient Data Confidentiality

XOVVA maintains a "clean-room" approach to data architecture. Any patient-specific data transmitted by practitioners for case consultation is subjected to aggressive anonymization protocols.

We adhere to cryptographic standards matching or exceeding HIPAA/HITECH and GDPR requirements. Data at rest is secured via AES-256 encryption, and data in transit utilizes TLS 1.3 protocols to ensure zero interception.

Cookies & Tracking

Our digital infrastructure employs minimal, utilitarian tracking mechanisms necessary for secure session management and platform analytics.

Essential Tokens

Required for professional portal authentication and secure state maintenance. Cannot be disabled.

Analytical Telemetry

Aggregated, non-identifiable interaction metrics to improve portal architecture. Opt-out available.

Your Rights

Licensed practitioners maintain absolute sovereignty over their credentialing and interaction data housed within the XOVVA ecosystem.

  • Right to Access: Request a comprehensive export of your professional profile data.
  • Right to Rectification: Immediate correction of licensing or contact discrepancies.
  • Right to Erasure: Complete architectural purge of your digital footprint, subject to regulatory retention mandates for pharmacovigilance.
